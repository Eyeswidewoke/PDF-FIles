Secondary Opportunities Fund III. LP 
Appendix 7 Board of Directors of the General Partner 
It is currently proposed that the following individuals will comprise the board of directors of the General Partner 
Alan Turner is a Cayman attorney originally from the UK who has been in practice in the Cayman Islands for over 25 
years. He is the managing partner of Turners and is also the managing director of Turners Management Ltd. a Cayman-
based corporate management business which provides registered office services and directors to Cayman registered 
entities. Alan is a director of multiple Cayman entities and he has over 10 years' experience providing director services 
to complex Cayman based entities. He attends board meetings of various Cayman entities on a regular basis and 
frequently acts as chairperson of such meetings. Alan also has many years of restructuring experience. 
Ian Ross is a Chartered Accountant and has been a member of The Institute of Chartered Accountants of Scotland since 
1976. Mr Ross left Scotland in 1977 and spent the next 10 years working in the Middle East as Senior Manager for 
Deloitte. He specialised in the audit of financial institutions and was a member of the team advising the Ruler of Abu 
Dhabi on his worldwide investments. Mr Ross accepted the position of Financial Controller of Walkers in the Cayman 
Islands in 1986 and remained there until 2003. Mr Ross has since 2003 worked with Turners and Tumers Management 
Ltd. He acts as a director of several Cayman based entities and his extensive auditing and accounting experience has 
proved very valuable to client entitles 
Kirsten Le Pape graduated with an Accounting degree from Averett University, Virginia, USA in 1991. In 2007, Kirsten 
joined Turners Management as Senior Accountant. She oversees the corporate management for clients of Turners 
Management Ltd. Kirsten's daily duties includes liaising with clients who maintain their registered office in the Cayman 
Islands, providing corporate secretary and director services, preparing minutes for annual general and statutory meetings 
and filing returns. Kirsten's experience as an accountant has proved invaluable to clients for whom she provides director 
services. 
Lawrence Edwards is founder and managing director of Delta Group Limited (www.dettagroup.ky). a Cayman Islands 
headquartered investment management and financial services group. Delta Group provides asset management and 
advisory, corporate finance, and fiduciary services to the alternative investment industry and includes a specialist division 
focused on the secondary, special opportunity and distressed market sectors. During his career Lawrence has acted in a 
management or fiduciary capacity for over 200 investment clients and overseen the retum of approximately US$6 billion 
in capital. Prior to founding Delta Group he had an international advisory career with PricewaterhouseCoopers for 17 
years. latterly as co-head of their corporate finance and advisory practice in the Cayman Islands. Lawrence is a Fellow of 
the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants and a qualified member of the Association of Business Recovery 
Professionals and the International Association of Restructuring, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Professionals. He is a 
resident in the Cayman Islands.
