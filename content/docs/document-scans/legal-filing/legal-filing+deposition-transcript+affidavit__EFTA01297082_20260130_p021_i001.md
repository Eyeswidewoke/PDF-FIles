Even if this Court were to find Prosser's arguments not waived, however, the motion is 
denied as a matter of law. Upon review of the evidence submitted to the jury, this Court 
finds Trustee Carroll submitted sufficient evidence regarding Jeffrey Prosser's ownership 
of the assets he fraudulently transferred to his wife to support the verdict in this case. In 
addition, the Court finds Jeffrey Prosser's insolvency at the time of each transfer is not an 
essential element of Trustee Carroll's claims.
