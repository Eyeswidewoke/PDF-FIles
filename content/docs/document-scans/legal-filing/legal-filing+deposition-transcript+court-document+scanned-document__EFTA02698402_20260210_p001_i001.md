THE BERN DYNAMIC LLP 
The Purpose 
The Bern Dynamic provides personal, discrete and highly customized advice to a select number of international business leaders 
who will benefit from the knowledge and network of Bern. 
Bern offers clients world-class, senior experts with global reach, along with the experience and ability to provide advice across the 
broadest range of issues, whether geopolitical, economic or business. Bern's advisers have been chosen for their experience in 
dealing with the complex, dynamic issues faced by top executives and for their ability to deliver excellent counsel. Bern's advisors 
will either work individually, in pairs or collectively, as clients wish. 
In tandem with its advisory role, The Bern Dynamic will use its unique international network to benefit its clients. 
Bern can create, manage and execute the ambitions of investors and entrepreneurs. In addition to its long-standing experience in 
established markets, Bern has developed a specialist focus on high growth markets in Asia, South America. Africa and the Middle 
East. 
In particular. Bern will seek out opportunities with, and between, high growth markets and its expertise will ensure the perfect 
entry and landing for the client. 
The Way 
While technology allows for round•the-clock, virtual connection. The Bern Dynamic believes that the power of personal 
relationships remains as important as ever. Each client will be assigned a dedicated member of Bern's executive team with whom 
they can consult, in confidence, to choose which advisor or advisors will provide the most relevant counsel. From its offices in 
London the Bern executive team will be available 24 hours a day and 7 days a week to deal with any requests that may arise and 
to provide confidential and rapid solutions and contacts. 
Bern will provide forums to give clients and advisors opportunities to meet and discuss current affairs and challenges or specific 
investment and business opportunities. More informal occasions will be organized to encourage Bern members to learn from each 
other's personal and professional experiences. 
The Bern Dynamic will be retained by its clients • individuals and organizations • on an annual basis. 
In addition. The Bern Dynamic will also offer a supplementary range of services: 
• 
Macro diligence: Bern will provide advice to enhance a client's awareness of all political, social and financial issues that 
may affect an investment or business decision. Bern's advice will balance opportunities and risks to help clients make 
their best choices. 
Bespoke advice: Bern will provide bespoke advice to boards or executive teams, drawing from its own advisory group 
and, where necessary, bringing in additional external experts. Bern's close relationships with top academic institutions 
and companies can bring together leading scholars and researchers to provide its clients with excellent, precisely 
directed leadership and advice. 
• 
Similarly. Bern can design and execute -Master Classes" for individual leaders or executive teams depending on the 
client's needs. 
• 
Bern has longstanding relationships with leading service providers in many regions of the world and will help clients to 
connect with such facilitators as and when needed. 
This Result 
The Bern Dynamic will connect leading businesses in the high growth markets to each other as well as to the established markets 
and nurture a new global business infrastructure for the benefit of all its clients.
