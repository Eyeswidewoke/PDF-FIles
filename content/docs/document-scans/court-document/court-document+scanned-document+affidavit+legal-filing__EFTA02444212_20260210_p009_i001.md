This Debenture may be amended, modified or

provision of this Debenture or of any breach or violation of this Debenture or any provision 
hereof shall be effective except to the extent expressly set forth in writing that is signed by the

arisen or the breach or default waived has occurred, and any waiver may be conditional. No 
waiver of any breach or violation of any agreement or provision herein contained shall be deemed 
a waiver of any preceding or succeeding breach or violation thereof nor of any other agreement or 
provision herein contained. No waiver or extension of time for performance of any obligation or 
act shall be deemed a waiver or extension of the time for performance of any other obligation or 
act.
