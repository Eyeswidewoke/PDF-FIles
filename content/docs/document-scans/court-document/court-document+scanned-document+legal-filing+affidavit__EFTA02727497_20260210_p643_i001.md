VII. 
HELICOPTER RELATED PROVISIONS 
1_ Type Design and Production Approval - The S-76 helicopter and all of its variants has 
been granted Type Certification by the U.S. Federal Aviation Administration ("FAA") 
under Federal Aviation Regulation ("FAR") Title 14 CFR Part 21. The certification basis 
of the S-76 as defined by Type Certificate HINE, is FAR Title 14 CFR Part 29, 
"Airworthiness Standards: Transport Category Rotorcraft". Manufacture of the S-76C-H-
helicopter is approved under Sikorsky's Production Certificate PC-105 granted in 
accordance with FAR Title 14 CFR Part 21. Sikorsky as Type Certificate Holder, in the 
course and conduct of its business may at its sole discretion, seek FAA approval for the 
manufacture of the Helicopter under Sikorsky's Type Certificate at any facility approved 
for such manufacture by FAA.

Operating Manual and one copy each of the Sikorsky Maintenance Manual, Illustrated 
Parts Catalog, Wiring Manual, Engine Operating and Maintenance Manual and Engine
