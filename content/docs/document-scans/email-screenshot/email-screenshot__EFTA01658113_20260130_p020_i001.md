FBI Investigating 2019 Murders In New Mexico. 
The Taos (NM) News (7/23, 30K) reports that the FBI is continuing its investigation into the 
murders of Antonio "Tony" Romero and his son, Buck Romero, who were "found murdered at a

homicides."
