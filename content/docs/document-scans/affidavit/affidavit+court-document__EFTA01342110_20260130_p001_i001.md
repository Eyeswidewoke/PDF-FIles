FOUR HUNDRED SEVENTY THOUSAND and 00/100 ($470,000) DOLLARS and other

grant, sell, convey and release unto the party of the second part, the heirs

simple absolute.

and improvements thereon that may presently exist, described as 
LITTLE. ST. JAMES ISLAND 
No. 6B Red Hook Quarter 
U.S. Virgin Islands 
TOGETHER with the appurtenances thereunto belonging and all the 
estate, title and rights of the party of the first part in and to said 
premises. 
SUBJECT, HOWEVER, to zoning regulations, to covenants, restrictions 
and easements of record, to Virgin Islands laws and to United States laws. 
TO HAVE AND TO HOLD the premises herein granted unto the party 
of the second part, the heirs, successors and assigns of the party of the 
second part forever, in fee simple absolute. 
the party of the first part covenants and warrants as follows: 
That said party of the first part is seized of the said 
premises in fee simple, and has good right to convoy same; 
That the party of the second part shall quietly enjoy the 
said premises; 
That the said premises are free from encumbrances. 
as aforesaid; 
That the party of the first part will execute or procure 
any further necessary assurance of the title to said 
premises; and 
That said party of the first part will forever warrant and 
defend the title to said premises.
