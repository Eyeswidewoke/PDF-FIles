12.11 Marketing Activity. 
From and after the date on which Buyer and Seller 
execute and deliver this Agreement until the earlier to occur of the Closing or the date of 
the termination of this Agreement in accordance with its terms (the "Blackout Period"), 
(i) neither Seller nor any agent or other person acting on its behalf will directly or 
indirectly solicit or entertain offers from, negotiate with or in any manner encourage,
