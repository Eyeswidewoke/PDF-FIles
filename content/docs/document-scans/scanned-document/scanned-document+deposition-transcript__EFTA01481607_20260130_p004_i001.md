Local author 
misses shortlist 
The shortlist for Britain's prestigious Man Booker Prize for Fiction has 
been announced, and Somerville's Claire Messud is not on it. Messud's novel 
"The Emperor's Children" was one of just 19 books to make the longlist. The 
six novels on the shortlist include: Kiran Desai 's "The Inheritance of 
Loss"; Kate Grenville 's "The Secret Lover"; M.J. Hyland 's "Carry Me Down"; 
Hisham Matar 's "In the Country of Men;" Edward St. Aubyn 's "Mother's 
Milk"; and Sarah Waters 's "The Night Watch."
