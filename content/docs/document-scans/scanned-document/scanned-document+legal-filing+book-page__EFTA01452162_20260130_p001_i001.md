FEEDER FUNDS. 
NO ACTION HAS BEEN OR WILL BE TAKEN IN THE KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA THAT WOULD 
PERMIT A PUBLIC OFFERING OF ANY INTERESTS IN THE FEEDER FUNDS IN THE KINGDOM OF 
SAUDI ARABIA. ANY INTERESTS IN THE FEEDER FUNDS MAY ONLY BE OFFERED AND SOLD IN 
THE KINGDOM OF SAUDI ARABIA THROUGH PERSONS AUTHORIZED TO DO SO IN ACCORDANCE 
WITH ARTICLE 4 (PRIVATE PLACEMENT REQUIREMENTS) OF THE INVESTMENT FUNDS 
REGULATIONS ISSUED BY THE CMA PURSUANT TO THE REGULATIONS FOLLOWING A 
NOTIFICATION TO THE CMA UNDER THE REGULATIONS. INVESTORS SHOULD NOTE THAT 
ARTICLE 4(G) OF THE REGULATIONS PLACES RESTRICTIONS ON SECONDARY MARKET 
ACTIVITY WITH RESPECT TO INTERESTS IN THE FEEDER FUNDS ACQUIRED BY INVESTORS. THE 
OFFEROR WILL NOT RECOGNIZE ANY RESALE OR OTHER TRANSFER, OR ATTEMPTED RESALE 
OR OTHER TRANSFER, MADE OTHER THAN IN COMPLIANCE WITH THE ABOVEMENTIONED 
RESTRICTION.

FINANCIAL INVESTMENT SERVICES AND CAPITAL MARKETS ACT OF KOREA CFSCMAD.

ELIGIBILITY OF ANY RECIPIENTS OF THIS MEMORANDUM TO ACQUIRE THE INTERESTS UNDER
