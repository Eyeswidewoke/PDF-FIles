1. Authorization, The undersigned hereby requests and authorizes The Chase Manhattan Bank 
(the "Bank:-) to accept and act upon any instructions ("Instructions") delivered by telephone or telex 
or written or facsimile transmission given or purportedly given by a person or persons authorized to 
transact business with the Bank as shown on the corporate resolution or other evidence or advice of 
authority from time to time maintained in the records of the bank.

unauthorized person and agrees that the Bank will have no liability or responsibility for acting in 
accordance with any Instructions, whether or not genuine or duly authorized. The Bank shall have no 
liability or responsibility for any misunderstandings arising out of any telephone Instructions.
