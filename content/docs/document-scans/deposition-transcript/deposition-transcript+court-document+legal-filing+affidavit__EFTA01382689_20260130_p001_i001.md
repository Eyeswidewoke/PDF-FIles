Network Solutions 
We provide a range of network solutions to business and financial institutions clients. Our EFT Network Solutions manage debit card 
and account processing solutions and facilitate EFT commerce, such as PIN debit. PIN-less debit, and ATM transactions. Our Stored Value 
Network Solutions facilitate stored value commerce, such as (1) closed-loop prepaid transactions, which are initiated by various types of prepaid 
cards issued by enterprises, such as retailers, that issue enterprise-branded cards that can generally be used only at the enterprise issuing the card or 
account, and (2) open-loop prepaid transactions, which arc initiated by various types of prepaid cards issued by a bank and carry a network 
association brand, such as Visa or MasterCard. enabling them to be used at multiple merchant locations.
