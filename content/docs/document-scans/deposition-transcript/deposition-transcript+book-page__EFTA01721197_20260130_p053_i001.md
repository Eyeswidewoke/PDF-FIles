Jose had received a spinal tap the night before.

"He says he wants the massage," said one

We approached the bed. Both of us stood on the

Jose was letting out the primal moan of some-
one who had not been touched for a long time, 
atthe point in his life when he most desperately 
needed to be touched. 
Subtly, Jose pressed his back up into my hands. 
I watched the family members lean forward, lift-

Slowly, I stroked down Jose's back, then back

The family members stared hard at our hands. 
We were not wearing rubber gloves because 
we knew AIDS was not transmitted by skin-to-
skin contact. Everyone should have known it,
