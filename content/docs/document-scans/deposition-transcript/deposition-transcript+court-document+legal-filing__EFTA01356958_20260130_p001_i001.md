ANNEX FOR NON-EEA BUSINESS 
1. This Annex to the Terms of Business (Annex) 
applies to business with or on behalf of a Client 
carried on by (a) DBAG from a Non-EEA 
Branch or (b) a Non-EEA Affiliate of DBAG that 
is booked to an EEA Branch, (Non-EEA 
Business)} 
2. The Terms of Business, as modified by this 
Annex, apply to: 
a) Non-EEA Branches in respect of Non-
EEA Business carried on by those Non-
EEA Branches; and 
b) An EEA Branch of DBAG in respect of 
Non-EEA Business that is carried on by 
a Non-EEA Branch or a Non-EEA 
Affiliate of DBAG but booked to such 
EEA Branch of DBAG (and business 
may be regarded as carried on by a Non-
EEA Branch or a Non-EEA Affiliate of 
DBAG for these purposes even if it is 
booked to an EEA Branch of DBAG). 
3. For the purposes of this Annex: 
a) EEA means European Economic 
Area. 
b) EEA Branch means a branch of 
DBAG in the EEA, or, if the UK is 
not in the EEA at the relevant time, 
EEA and UK 
c) Non-EEA Affiliate means an entity 
in the DB Group that is incorporated 
outside the EEA, or, if the UK is not 
in the EEA at the relevant time, EEA 
and UK. 
d) Non-EEA Branch means a branch 
of DBAG outside the EEA, or, if the 
UK is not in the EEA at the relevant
