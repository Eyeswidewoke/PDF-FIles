12. Concentration of Credit Risk 
The Company relies on three food distributors for the majority of its beef and grocery purchases. However, the products purchased through 
the distributors arc widely available at similar prices from multiple distributors. The Company does not anticipate any risk to the business in the 
event that one or both of these distributors is no longer available to provide their goods or givi=. However, a change in suppliers could 
potentially result in increased costs.
