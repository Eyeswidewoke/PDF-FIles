Sales and marketing 
We attract the majority of our users through word-of-mouth and other free channels. In addition, many of our brands rely on paid customer acquisition for a 
significant percentage of their users. Our online marketing activities generally consist of purchasing banner and other display advertising, search engine

related public relations efforts, as well as events.
