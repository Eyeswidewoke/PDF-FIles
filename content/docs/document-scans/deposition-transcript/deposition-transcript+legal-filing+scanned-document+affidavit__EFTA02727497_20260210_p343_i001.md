5. Presentation of Completion Services for Acceptance — Upon the completion of the 
Completion Services, the Deluxe Executive Helicopter shall be presented for technical 
acceptance of the work performed at the Completion Center on the Scheduled Presentation 
Date Completion Services. Upon Buyer's request for an inspection and demonstration flight 
of the Deluxe Executive Helicopter, Sikorsky shall conduct a demonstration flight of the 
Deluxe Executive Helicopter, at the Completion Center during such inspection and

reasonably be necessary. Buyer's obligation to accept the Deluxe Executive Helicopter is 
conditioned upon and subject to Buyer being satisfied that the Completion Services have 
been satisfactorily completed and that the Helicopter as previously accepted by Buyer, is in

damage incurred.
