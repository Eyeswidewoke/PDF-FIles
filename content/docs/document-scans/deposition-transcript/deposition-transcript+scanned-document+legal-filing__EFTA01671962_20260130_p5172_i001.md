Applicable for Residents of Colorado 
Effective January 1, 2014, all definitions, terms and provisions within the Policies wherever appearing and denoting a 
marital relationship or family relationship arising out of marriage will include parties to a civil union established in the State 
of Colorado according to Colorado law and their families. The terms that mean or refer to family relationships arising from 
a marriage, such as "family ", "immediate family", "dependent", "children", "next of kin", "relative", "beneficiary", "survivor" 
and any other such terms include family relationships created by a civil union established according to Colorado law.
