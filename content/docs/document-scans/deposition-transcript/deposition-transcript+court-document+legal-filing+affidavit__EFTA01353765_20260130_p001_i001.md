Operational Rules. 
For purposes of 7.2.1 and this 7.2.2:

If distributions to which a Defaulting Partner otherwise would have been entitled have

(b)

7.2.1; 
(c) 
Distributions made to any Partner's predecessors in interest shall be treated as having 
been made to such Partner; 
(d) 
The amount of any distribution of securities in kind shall be equal to the fair market value 
of such securities at the time of distribution: and 
(e) 
If them are Defaulting Partners, distributions shall be modified to the extent required by 
Article 6; and references in this Article 7 to "Partners" and to "Limited Partner" shall be 
modified accordingly.
