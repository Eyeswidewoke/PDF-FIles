exchange or regulated trading facility in Switzerland. This prospectus has been prepared without regard to the disclosure standards for issuance

the SIX Listing Rules or the listing rules of any other stock exchange or regulated trading facility in Switzerland. Neither this prospectus nor any

Switzerland.

or approved by any Swiss regulatory authority. In particular, this prospectus will not be filed with, and the offer of securities will not be supervised 
by, the Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority FINMA, and the offer of securities has not been and will not be authorized under the Swiss

schemes under the CISA does not extend to acquirers of securities.
