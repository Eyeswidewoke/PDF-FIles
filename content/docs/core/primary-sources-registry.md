## How to Use This Registry

1. **When citing:** Reference sources from this registry first
2. **When adding:** Follow the format below; verify access URL works
3. **Reliability tiers:** Tier 1 (official/primary) > Tier 2 (reputable journalism) > Tier 3 (analysis)
4. **Cross-reference:** Link to relevant Claim Ledger entries

## Reliability Tier Definitions

| Tier | Definition | Examples |
|------|------------|----------|
| **Tier 1** | Primary sources — official documents, sworn testimony, direct recordings | Government reports, court filings, transcripts, video |
| **Tier 2** | Reputable journalism — verified reporting from established outlets | NYT, WaPo, AP, Reuters, major investigative series |
| **Tier 3** | Analysis & secondary — books, documentaries, expert commentary | Academic papers, documentaries, op-eds by experts |
