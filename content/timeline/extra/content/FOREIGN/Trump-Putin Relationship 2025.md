## The Central Mystery: Why Is Trump So Friendly to Putin?

Yet Trump consistently:

This stance is **unprecedented in modern American history** and demands explanation.

**Theory**: Trump and Putin are actively cooperating to reshape global order toward authoritarian dominance.

## Historical Comparison: How Unusual Is This?

### Reagan and the Soviet Union

**Context**: Cold War, Soviet Union as existential threat

**Reagan's Approach**:
- Called USSR "evil empire"
- Massively increased defense spending to pressure Soviets
- Supported anti-Soviet resistance movements worldwide
- Negotiated from position of strength (while maintaining military pressure)

**Outcome**: Soviet economic collapse, end of Cold War, victory for democracy

### Obama and Russia

**Context**: Post-Cold War, attempted "reset" in relations

**Obama's Approach**:
- Attempted diplomatic engagement initially
- Responded to Crimea annexation with **strong sanctions**
- Maintained support for NATO
- Expelled Russian diplomats after election interference

**Outcome**: Relationship deteriorated but U.S. maintained principled stance defending international law

### Trump and Russia

**Context**: Russia invaded European country, attacked U.S. elections, assassinated dissidents on foreign soil

**Trump's Approach**:
- Praised Putin's leadership
- Questioned U.S. intelligence agencies
- Threatened to withdraw from NATO
- Signaled ending support for Ukraine
- Pursued personal financial opportunities in Russia

**Outcome**: Russian strategic position strengthened, American alliances weakened, authoritarian model normalized

**The Verdict**: Trump's approach is **unprecedented** in its alignment with an adversary's interests over American and allied interests.
