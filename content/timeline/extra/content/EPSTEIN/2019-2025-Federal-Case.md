## 2019: Arrest & Death

### July 6, 2019 - Arrest

- Arrested at Teterboro Airport (NJ)
- Returning from Paris
- SDNY jurisdiction (not Florida - different from 2008 case)

### July 9, 2019 - SDNY Press Release

**Official Charges** (per SDNY DOJ announcement):
1. **Sex trafficking of minors**
2. **Conspiracy to commit sex trafficking of minors**

**Alleged conduct period**: 2002-2005

**Evidence cited**:
- Victims from 2002-2005
- New York and Florida locations
- Detailed recruitment scheme

### July 23, 2019 - First Incident

- Found injured in cell
- Marks on neck
- Cellmate questioned
- Placed on suicide watch (briefly)

### July 25, 2019 - Bail Denied

- Judge: flight risk
- $500M+ in assets
- Private plane, international connections
- Victims' safety concerns

### August 10, 2019 - Death

**Found**: ~6:30 AM in Special Housing Unit (SHU), MCC Manhattan
**Official Ruling**: Suicide by hanging (NYC Medical Examiner)

### DOJ Inspector General Documented Failures

The **DOJ OIG report** cites **systemic breakdowns**:

| Failure | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Staffing failures** | Inadequate personnel |
| **Cameras not functional** | Security footage unavailable |
| **Officers falsified records** | Logs showed checks that didn't happen |
| **Required rounds not performed** | Epstein not checked for hours |
| **Cellmate removed** | Night before death, no explanation |

### Independent Review

- **Dr. Michael Baden** (hired by Epstein family) questioned official findings
- Noted injuries more consistent with homicide than suicide
- Official ruling unchanged

### "Mysterious" Element

The documented failures (cameras, guards, falsified records) are the factual basis for ongoing suspicion - not speculation, but official findings of institutional breakdown.
