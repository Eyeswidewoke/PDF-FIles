Legal + Institutional Milestones (Quick View)

  • 2007-2008 NPA architecture and later CVRA findings remain the foundational legal controversy.
  • 2019 federal charges and 2021-2022 Maxwell verdict/sentencing are the core criminal adjudication milestones.
  • DOJ OIG custody findings, banking-regulator actions, and 2025-2026 statutory release mechanics are the institutional accountability backbone.
  • Post-EFTA, the core disputes are now process quality: completeness, context metadata, redaction integrity, and correction discipline.

Unresolved Questions

  • Completeness: did production reach full scope in a stable and searchable form?
  • Redaction integrity: can victim privacy and mass transparency be reconciled at scale without repeat leakage?
  • Interpretation: how should "named in files" be context-labeled to prevent false inference?
  • Uncharged third parties: what would be required for formal prosecutorial action vs. declination?
  • Banking facilitation boundaries: what controls failed, when, and with what reporting consequences?
  • International asymmetry: how should cross-border reputational fallout be handled when adjudication is incomplete?
